Experimental Design

Kafe Kultura

Coffee and Museum in one cup placed on a surface of urban life

The idea to realize a project of an experimental café in Minsk has emerged at the moment of working on the National pavilion at the Venice Biennale. The idea was to invent a space with the conception of a café at the museum, so that there would be the feeling of a museum and a café at one and the same time. To create the prototype of a conceptual museum.
